Photography Assignment

The goal of this assignment was to capture photographs and edit them after using the photoshop software. We had to demostrate the use of the composition rules that we earned in class and edit the photos in photoshop to imporve the image.

I firtly decided what photographs I wanted to capture. most of the chosen photographs were static, so by using various composition rules capturing them was managable. One photo which was a little harder to take was of my dog as he very rarely stays still.

What I Learned: I learned about the importance of composition rules in terms of photography and what important part they have to play in capturing photographs. I learned that photoshop can be a tedious software espically when using it for the first time, so I would recommended following tutoril to familiar yourself with the layout before diving into it.
